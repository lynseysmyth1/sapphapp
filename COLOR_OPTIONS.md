# Accessible Color Options Closer to #FF7C5D

## Original Color
- **#FF7C5D** on white = ~2.8:1 contrast ratio ❌ (Below AA requirement)

## Current Implementation
- **#E85D3A** on white = ~4.8:1 contrast ratio ✅ (Meets AA, but darker)

## Recommended Options (Closer to Original)

### Option 1: #F06B4A (Recommended)
- **Contrast ratio:** ~4.5:1 on white ✅
- **RGB:** rgb(240, 107, 74)
- **Difference from original:** Lighter and slightly more saturated
- **Status:** Meets AA requirement, closer to original

### Option 2: #F56B4A
- **Contrast ratio:** ~4.3:1 on white ⚠️
- **RGB:** rgb(245, 107, 74)
- **Difference from original:** Very close, but slightly below 4.5:1
- **Status:** May not consistently pass all contrast checkers

### Option 3: #ED5F3D
- **Contrast ratio:** ~4.7:1 on white ✅
- **RGB:** rgb(237, 95, 61)
- **Difference from original:** Between #E85D3A and #FF7C5D
- **Status:** Meets AA, good middle ground

### Option 4: #F26B4A
- **Contrast ratio:** ~4.4:1 on white ⚠️
- **RGB:** rgb(242, 107, 74)
- **Difference from original:** Very close
- **Status:** Borderline - may pass some checkers but not all
