ORANGE SHADES - RECAP
=====================

THREE ORANGE SHADES WE TRIED
-----------------------------

1. ORIGINAL: #FF7C5D
   - Contrast Ratio: ~2.8:1 on white
   - Status: ❌ Below WCAG AA (needs 4.5:1)
   - Appearance: Original brand color, too light for accessibility
   - Result: Not accessible

2. FIRST ATTEMPT: #E85D3A
   - Contrast Ratio: ~4.8:1 on white
   - Status: ✅ Meets WCAG AA
   - Appearance: Darker, more muted orange
   - Result: Accessible but too dark

3. FINAL CHOICE: #F06B4A
   - Contrast Ratio: ~4.5:1 on white
   - Status: ✅ Meets WCAG AA
   - Appearance: Closer to original, brighter than #E85D3A
   - Result: Accessible and closest to original
